About the Training

This hands-on workshop is designed for Nigerian school teachers to master the use of AI in teaching.

  • Save hours preparing lesson plans
  • Convert handwritten notes to editable documents
  • Create interactive quizzes
  • Predict exam trends from past questions
  • Deepen subject knowledge and improve teaching confidence

Training Modules

Module 1: Lesson Planning with AI

Generate lesson plans, notes, and teaching materials using AI and verified textbook references.

Module 2: AI OCR for Handwritten Documents

Convert handwritten scripts or questions into editable documents for faster printing.

Module 3: Fun Quizzes & Interactive Learning

Design fun quizzes, games, and class activities to make lessons engaging.

Module 4: Past Question Analysis & Prediction

Analyze WAEC, NECO, and JAMB past questions to identify patterns and likely questions.

Module 5: Enhancing Subject Mastery

Deepen your knowledge of challenging topics to teach students confidently and clearly.

Module 6: Revision Notes & Summaries

Create ready-to-print revision materials to help students excel.

Module 7: Prompting Techniques & Best Practices

Learn how to give AI the right instructions for maximum efficiency, including examples relatable to Nigerian pupils.
